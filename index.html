Frequently Asked Questions

How does 100 Day Dress make you more productive?

Wearing the same outfit every day is an old productivity trick practiced by the likes of Steve Jobs, Hillary Clinton, Vera Wang, Mark Zuckerberg, Barak Obama and many more. They have all said that making one less decision in the morning turns their minds to what really matters - being creative and being productive. Wearing the same outfit is a lot easier though, when it is trasformable. And that is where 100 Day Dress comes it.

Wear the same dress, really?

No worries, the dress is so transformable that you will not feel like you are wearing the same thing. It can two colors, multiple sleeve length options, adjustable skirt length, two necklines, and many more options. For easy hygiene maintenance we recommend you have two dresses, so always have a clean one for the next day.

How many transformations can I do?

In principle - endless. You can keep changing your designs until you run out of ideas and if you do, there will always be more in our app shared by other users. We do guarantee that there are at least 100 easy-to-make transformations.

What is ThunderClap? And why are you using it?

ThunderClap lets you "crowdspeak." With it you and us can say one since all at the same time. We care about being productive, so we want the world to know that there is a new way to be more productive- wear transformable clothes. Thunderclap is the best way to make sure everyone say the same thing at once - then it is more likely to reach the people who need to hear it.

How long can I wear this dress?

As long as you like. We made this dress durable so it can withstand years of wear and transformations.

Can I wear it with a bra?

Yes. There are many designs that cover the back completely. Some designs call for a strapless bra, and those with the back open entirely would be better off with  adhesive cups.

Can I wear the dress in cold weather?

Yes, because the dress has layers. Plus, it allows for a layer underneath, something like a tube or a tank top.

Is is suitable for all ages?

Yes, the dress can be made to look business-like, party-like, conservative and seductive.

Does the dress look good of different body shapes?

Yes. We used the most universally flattering design elements, like the boat neck, the V-neck, and lowered waistline, so that no matter your body share the dress enhances your figure.

Isn't transformable dress an old idea?

Yes, there have been transformable dresses before. None were suitable for both business and leisure. If they were, we would have just worn them instead of making a new one. The 100 day dress really is the first one to cover most life situations.
