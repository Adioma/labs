The Instructor

Anna Vital is an information designer. She helps companies around the workd to convert their data and knowledge into memorable graphics.

Her infographics have been published by Entrepreneur Magazine, Fast Company, Inc Magazine, VentureBeat, Virgin, and World Economic Forum. She creates editorial pieces for Funders and Founders as well as for clients. Her infogrpahics have been named as Top Infographics of the Year in 2014 and 2013 by Entrepreneur Magazine.
